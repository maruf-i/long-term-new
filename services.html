Our Services

Main Services

Liaison Interpreting

If you have any Mandarin speaking clients/friends who come to the UK, we provide the normal interpreting service just to accompany them to shopping, sight-seeing, collect/send to airport, or other informal events (no documents needed to prepare).

Consecutive Interpreting

Consecutive interpreting is one common type of interpretation, interpreter translate listen to what a person says, usually a few sentences at a time, then make notes, translate the content after the original speaker has finished.

Simultaneous Interpreting

Simultaneous interpreting service mostly used for formal meetings, normal need 2 interpreters work closely together, in a booth. The interpreters translates the message simultaneously from the original speakers.
