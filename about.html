About us

Who We Are

Talking about us is not enough, we want to know more about you! We understand one is in a foreign country, his/her voice is vital to be heard. Long-Term Exclusive Interpreting Is determined to become a leading interpreting company to provide premium services to you and your business clients as well as those from China and the UK with an international characteristic. With the growing numbers of international trades and investments between Great Britain and China, companies from both of the countries are seeking to develop overseas market in the counterpart.

Long-Term Exclusive Interpreting has a group of qualified Chinese speaking interpreters, which could help you and your clients to overcome language barriers. Most of our staff are from China. We know how Chinese think, how to talk to them, how to make good "Guanxi" to them. We want to narrow the distance between you and the Chinese market.

只写关于我们并不足够,我们更想了解您。我们充分理解到当您来到异国的那一刻,您的声音需要被别人听到.龙腾陪同翻译有信心成为一个专业的口译公司专门为您打造优质翻译服务. 如今中英之间的贸易往来更为频繁, 无论是中国公司还是英国当地公司,都非常迫切寻找跨国合作伙伴,让对方听到自己声音.

龙腾陪同翻译致力提供优秀翻译人才为您客服语言及文化障碍, 我们的翻译人员大部分来自中国并且在英国有多年生活经验.我们清楚如何与英国人交流, 如何帮您与英国人拉近距离,顺利完成项目.

Founder

Joanna came to the UK 7 years ago. She could not speak English properly and felt nervous when she talked to other non-Chinese speakers in the first year in the UK. After a few years studying in the UK, she found doing business was fascinating. She attended more than 50 events include tradeshows, workshops and other entrepreneur networks. She realized an increasing number of Chinese tourists and businesses come to the UK in the recent year, which made her decide running an interpreting company can help Chinese visitors overcome the language barriers, also can help UK companies reach their potential clients more easily.

年前来到英国,当时她只有18岁,英语水平不高,跟外国人交流非常吃力.经过了几年在英国学习,并且在工作经历中她产生了对创业有浓厚的兴趣. 她试过在一年内参加过大大小小的活动将近有50场,包括了大型展会, 学习班,创业社交活动等.她意识到中国企业过来英国交流比往年更为密切, 决定成立龙腾翻译公司是基于她想帮助中国企业在英国接洽项目更顺利,她想为中国与英国交流尽一份力.
